## Construction Details

| Operation | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Shoulder Seam | 4-thread overlock, 12 SPI |
| Neck Binding | Cover stitch, 3-needle |
| Back Neck Tape | 15mm twill tape, cover stitched |
| Sleeve Attach | 4-thread overlock, 12 SPI |
| Sleeve Hem | 1" double fold, cover stitch |
| Side Seam | 4-thread overlock, 12 SPI |
| Bottom Hem | 1" double fold, cover stitch |
| Side Vent | 1.5" opening, bartack reinforced |

### Stitch Types

- **401** - Chain stitch for seaming
- **504** - 3-thread overlock for finishing
- **516** - 5-thread safety stitch for seams
- **602** - Cover stitch for hems

## Labels and Packaging

### Main Label
- Position: Center back neck, 1cm below neckline seam
- Attachment: Sewn on both short ends
- Content: Brand logo

### Care Label
- Position: Left side seam, 3cm above hem
- Content: Fiber content, care instructions, country of origin, size

### Care Instructions
```
Machine wash cold
Tumble dry low
Do not bleach
Iron low heat
Do not dry clean
```

### Packaging
- Fold: Retail fold with tissue
- Polybag: Individual, suffocation warning printed
- Carton: 50 units per carton

---

## Quality Standards

- All seams must be smooth with no puckering
- No loose threads longer than 3mm
- Color must match approved lab dip within Grade 4
- Measurements within stated tolerances
- No holes, stains, or fabric defects
- Print (if applicable) centered and registered
